BIOGRAPHY

Kayla Jacobs

Kayla Jacobs is an American singer/songwriter from Kansas, living in Los Angeles, California.

With her recent success in the House music genre, working with producer El Mukuka on songs like “Rainfall” and “Bottle of Loneliness”, you might be unaware of her expansive background as a jazz singer.

After years of performing sultry standards, and rhythmic Latin jazz in local Wichita bars and restaurants, she set off to study at Berklee College of Music in Boston, in order to pursue her passion for songwriting. “I always thought of myself as a songwriter first, and a singer second. It felt like my songs were meant to be heard, even if I wasn’t the one singing them.”

Luckily she is the one singing them. Because it’s her voice that beckons us to listen longer, and her lyrics that beg us to listen again. With a voice like Amy Winehouse, and a quirky creativity like Regina Spektor, her sound is powerful, seductive, and playful.
